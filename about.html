About Us

We’re building a student-friendly nutrition guide for Singaporean learners.

NutriGuide SG is designed to help users understand healthier eating in a simple, respectful, and practical way. The website is the front door to a larger project that your teammates can later extend with automation, AI assistance, and video support.

Our mission

To make healthy eating advice easier to access and easier to act on, especially for busy students who need fast, non-judgmental guidance.

What we are not

We are not a medical service. The site should stay within safe healthy eating education and avoid diagnosing conditions or giving unsafe advice.

Project direction

Built to fit the workflow your team described.

1

Clear homepage and about page

This site gives NutriGuide SG a polished public-facing identity before the AI layers are added.

2

Future AI integration

Your teammates can connect n8n, Google AI Studio, DeepSeek, and Synthesia after the core web experience is ready.

3

Safe, compliant guidance
