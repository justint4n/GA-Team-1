About NutriGuide

A student-built wellness brand shaped for real-world use.

NutriGuide combines nutrition education, approachable UX, and AI support into one experience. The goal is simple: help people make healthier food choices without making the process feel heavy.

Mission

Make healthy eating clearer

We focus on reducing information overload and turning nutrition guidance into small, understandable steps.

Brand

Calm, supportive, modern

The visual language uses fresh greens, rounded surfaces, and warm copywriting to feel reassuring instead of clinical.

Technology

Ready for AI workflows

BotPress powers the live assistant, while the site structure is prepared for future n8n workflows and content automation.

What the site communicates

Clear value, clear tone, and clear next steps.

This website presents NutriGuide as a product rather than a classroom prototype. The messaging explains the problem, the interface shows the experience, and the interactive elements reinforce how the solution works in practice.
